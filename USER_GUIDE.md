# User Guide

## Cashiering Workflow

1. Open POS Quick Sell.
2. Search or scan product.
3. Select customer (or keep Walk-In).
4. Apply optional discount.
5. Apply optional rewards redemption.
6. Enter payment mode and tendered amount.
7. Complete checkout.

## Payment Handling

- Single payment mode is supported.
- Split payment mode is supported.
- Payment allocation is normalized to invoice total.

## Rewards

- Points can be earned from product and service sales.
- Points can be redeemed at checkout.
- Expiry rules can remove old unconsumed points.
- History page tracks reward transactions.

## Reports

### Cashier Report

- Filter by date range and cashier.
- View invoice, payment mode, total, paid, and open values.
- Includes rewards summary metrics.

### Z Report

- Generate daily summary.
- View payment mode and cashier breakdown.
- Includes rewards summary metrics.
- Export as CSV or PDF.

### Rewards History

- Filter by date, staff, and customer.
- Export to CSV.
- Review earned, redeemed, available, and balance-after values.

## Permissions

Use role permissions to control who can:

- Operate cashiering
- View own vs all cashier reports
- View own vs all Z reports
- Manage rewards settings/history
